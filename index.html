About

18 year old Norwegian producer Alan Walker released his first official single in 2014 - the melodic electro house track ”Fade”. What started as a kid's journey into the digital world, driven by a desire to create and explore, has developed into one of the most exciting producers Norway will offer in 2016. Alan’s first release with Sony Music will be the single ”Faded”, a vocal version of ”Fade”, release date November 25th.
Growing up in the digital era, Alan found an early interest in computers which later turned into a fascination for programming and graphic design. In 2012 he started making music on his laptop, and with the help and feedback from his online friends his songs soon caught the attention of the record labels.
